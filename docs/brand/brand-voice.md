# AuditHalo Brand Voice & Messaging

## Tagline
**"Supervision compliance, handled."**

Alternatives:
- "Every hour. Every signature. Every audit."
- "Built for the board. Trusted by supervisors."

## Positioning Statement
AuditHalo is the clinical supervision compliance platform that gives licensed supervisors in NC, CA, TX, FL, and NY tamper-evident, board-ready documentation of every supervised hour — so that when the board calls, the answer is ready.

## Home Page Hero
**Primary (recommended):**
> Supervision records your board will never question.

Subhead: AuditHalo tracks supervised hours, captures e-signed session notes, and generates tamper-evident audit packages — built for LCMHCA, APCC, LPC-A, RMHCI, and LP-MHC supervisors.

## Voice Principles

### 1. Speak to the professional, not the problem
The supervisor is a credentialed expert. Address them as a peer with a workflow gap.
- DO: "You're already a rigorous clinician. AuditHalo makes sure your records reflect that."
- DON'T: "Stop using messy spreadsheets that put you at risk."

### 2. Name compliance anxiety without weaponizing it
Board audits are real. Acknowledge the stakes calmly — don't use fear as the primary lever.
- DO: "If your board requested your supervision records today, you'd have everything ready."
- DON'T: "One audit could cost you your license."

### 3. Be specific about the regulatory world
Use real credential names, real board names, real hour requirements.
- DO: "LCMHCA supervisees in NC need 3,000 hours. AuditHalo tracks every one against NCBLCMHC requirements."
- DON'T: "Track hours for your supervised clinicians in any state."

### 4. Respect the dual relationship
Frame the value to supervisors as protection; to supervisees as security and transparency.
- DO: "Your supervisees always know where they stand. You always know you're covered."
- DON'T: "Monitor your supervisees' progress."

### 5. Earned confidence, not cheerfulness
Clinical professionals distrust over-marketed products. Plain declarative sentences.
- DO: "Tamper-evident audit packages. Generated in one click."
- DON'T: "Wow, audits are SO easy now! 🎉"

## Sentence case in all headlines. Em-dashes for asides. No exclamation points.
